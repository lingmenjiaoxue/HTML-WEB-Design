Tech Recipe

Hardware

  • 1 Central Processing Unit (CPU)
  • 1 Motherboard
  • 2 or 4 sticks of Random Access Memory (RAM)
  • 1 or more Storage Drives (Hard Drive or Solid State Drive)
  • 1 Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)
  • 1 Power Supply Unit (PSU)
  • 1 Computer Case
  • 1 CPU Cooler
  • 1 or more Monitors
  • 1 Keyboard
  • 1 Mouse
  • 1 Operating System (like Windows 10 or Linux)

Build Instructions

  1. Prepare your workspace: Clear, clean,
    and static-free.
  2. Install the CPU into the motherboard.
  3. Attach the CPU cooler on top.
  4. Insert RAM sticks into the
    motherboard slots.
  5. Position the motherboard in the computer case.
  6. Mount the Power Supply Unit (PSU)
    in the case.
  7. Connect PSU to the motherboard and CPU.
  8. Install your storage drive
    in the case.
  9. Insert the Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)
    into its slot.
  10. onnect all components to the PSU.
  11. Manage cables for neatness
    and airflow.
  12. Install the operating system.
